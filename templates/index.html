About Us



How It Works


Bilingual and monolingual people can rate their experiences listening to foreign-accented speech in environments with varying levels of bubble noise, white noise, and quiet speech . And these rating will accurately predict sound level preference in noise perception using a machine learning model. Our motto is to improve the quality and intelligibility of a speech fragment in applications such as hearing aids, automatic speech recognition (ASR), mobile communication, etc.

Our Research


Speech intelligibility is currently measured by scoring how well a person can identify a speech signal. The results of such behavioral measures reflect neural processing of the speech signal but are also influenced by language processing, motivation, and memory. Our research work is Speech intelligibility Prediction from Behavioral Patterns by Using Supervised Machine Learning Algorithms for Bilingual & Monolingual Listeners with the help of speech stimuli data.
